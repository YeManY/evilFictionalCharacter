    Meruem is the main antagonist of the Chimera Ant Arc of the Hunter x Hunter anime and manga.

    Meruem is the King of the Chimera Ant species, Chimera Ants are a fictional species of Magical Beast that originate from The Dark Continent.

    Meruem has a humanoid body and is rather muscular and toned.

    Meruem's Nen type is that of a specialist.
